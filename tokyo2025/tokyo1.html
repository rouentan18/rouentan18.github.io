Tokyo 14/9/2025 - 21/9/2025

Published on September 21, 2025 by Rou En

This is my first time went to Tokyo, we get a good deal with Vietnam Airlines and we stay at Asakusa Tobu Hotel for the whole trip.

1st Day (14/09/2025)

We depart yesterday from Kuala Lumpur, KLIA 1 airport (19:25) to Ho Chi Minh, Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN) (20:35) for transit. Then fly from Ho Chi Minh (00:25) and reached Tokyo Narita airport (08:00)

After we arrive at Narita airport, we buy the "Welcome Suica" IC card using the machine. Then collect the Tokyo Metro Pass using the machine as well.

Our planning is to take the Keisei Narita Skyaccess from the airport to Asakusa, which are chaper and directly reach Asakusa. But we went to the wrong train, which heading to Ueno, luckily Ueno is consider quite near and convenient to reach Asakusa by train. We arrived at the Asakusa Tobu hotel around 10:30 am, we are lucky that staff offered us to have early check-in, feel refreshing after a shower especially after traveling for long hours.

Lunch we eat tonkatsu in asakusa

Then we take train to Ueno again, we plan our first day attractions focus at here.

  1. Ueno Zoo
  2. National Museum of Nature and Science
  3. Ameya-Yokocho Market
